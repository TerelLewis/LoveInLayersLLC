# Love In Layers LLC - Baking Calculator

A professional baking calculator and pricing tool designed to help bakers manage ingredients, scale recipes, and calculate appropriate pricing for their baked goods.

## Features

- **Ingredient Management**: Add, edit, and remove ingredients with quantities, units, and cost per unit
- **Serving Size Scaling**: Easily scale recipes up or down by adjusting the target servings
- **Real-time Calculations**: All quantities and costs update dynamically as you make changes
- **Cost & Pricing Calculator**: 
  - Track total ingredient costs
  - Add labor and overhead costs
  - Set profit margins
  - Get suggested selling prices
- **Export Functionality**: Export scaled recipes with pricing to text files
- **Local Storage**: Your recipes are saved automatically in your browser

## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser
2. Add your ingredients with their base quantities and costs
3. Adjust the target servings to scale the recipe
4. Set your labor, overhead costs, and desired profit margin
5. View the suggested selling price and expected profit

## Quick Start with Sample Recipe

Click "Load Sample Recipe" to see the calculator in action with a pre-configured vanilla cake recipe.

## Files

- `index.html` - Main application HTML
- `styles.css` - Application styling
- `calculator.js` - Application logic and calculations

## Usage Tips

- **Base Recipe Servings**: The original recipe yield (e.g., 12 cupcakes)
- **Target Servings**: The desired yield after scaling
- **Scale Factor**: Automatically calculated ratio (Target ÷ Base)
- **Cost per Unit**: The cost of one unit of the ingredient (e.g., cost per cup)
